CHOOSE YOUR ROOM

From roadside motel rooms to presidential casino suites, there are a plethora of different types of hotel rooms for the modern traveller to choose from. Whether you’re booking at a large brand or chain hotel, a boutique hotel, an all-inclusive resort, or another accommodation style, there’s a room type out there to fit everyone’s unique needs. In this guide, we take a deep dive into some of the most popular types of hotel rooms and what they include. We break down room types based on size, style, and services, and then answer some frequently asked questions about hotel room types.
